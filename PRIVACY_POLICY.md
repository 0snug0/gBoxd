# Privacy Policy

**Letterboxd for Google** — Last updated: March 2026

## What this extension does

This extension adds a "Letterboxd" button to Google's movie knowledge panel, allowing you to open a film's page on Letterboxd directly from Google Search.

## Data collection

This extension collects **no data whatsoever**.

- It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
- It does not track your searches, browsing history, or any user activity.
- It does not use cookies, analytics, or any third-party services.
- It does not make any network requests of its own.

## How it works

The extension reads the movie title and year displayed in Google's knowledge panel (only when a film panel is detected), constructs a Letterboxd URL client-side, and injects a button into the page. All processing happens entirely within your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

## Permissions

This extension requests no special browser permissions. It only runs on `https://www.google.com/search*` pages.

## Third-party services

Clicking the Letterboxd button will take you to [letterboxd.com](https://letterboxd.com), which has its own privacy policy. This extension has no affiliation with Letterboxd.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be committed to the public repository at [github.com/0snug0/letterboxd-google](https://github.com/0snug0/letterboxd-google).
